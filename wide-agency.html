Based in Paris, Wide is a digital agency specialized in customer experience. There are 200 employees from different businesses who work hand in hand. They work on data, design, technology, CRM and consulting issues. I’ve been working there for 2 years as an art director.

Atomic design

Before prototyping I’ve started to establish brand's guidelines. I have to choose the main colors, icons and typographies. With these elements I’ve created all the molecules which allowed me to bring masters templates. This creative process is an efficient way to easily update and upgrade a website.

The main challenge was to create a website meeting the needs of each professional discipline of the agency. After capturing the requirements of the different departments, we designed a modular environment allowing users to easily plug the information they wish.
